Who we are

Technology partners, not just service providers.

Integrated Technology Solutions (ITS) is a South African technology company dedicated to helping businesses and individuals leverage technology to achieve their goals.

We provide affordable, reliable and innovative IT services — from website development and hosting to technical support, branding and digital solutions. Our mission is to simplify technology so our clients can focus on growing their businesses.
